Campaign Strategy & Execution

This integrated campaign creates multiple touchpoints across Whisky Advocate's premium digital ecosystem. We'll build a custom Loco Tequila experience that lives on WhiskyAdvocate.com, then drive qualified traffic through seven synchronized marketing channels. Each element is designed to work together, creating a surround-sound effect that builds brand awareness, educates consumers, and drives purchase consideration.
